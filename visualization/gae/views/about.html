About OSSRank

With millions of open-source software and libraries available in the internet and many more getting added every day, It is not easy to have a overview of them.Nor do we know about their capability or their overall standard. OSSRank is our effort to contribute to open-source community by automatically
  1. Discovering open-source tools
  2. Classifying them in a specific(or multiple) category
  3. Collcting data about them from stackexchange & finding their social footprint in twitter
  4. Continueously evaluating them and tracking their growth over a timeline.
  5. Above all giving them suggetsive rank in their specific category
In future we plan to do much more adding sentiment analysis of the feeds in twitter, and add security analysis as part of our evaluation.
